Shared Skills

Break Age

Unleash the power of your equipped Devil Breaker to perform a devastating attack. The sheer power of this technique will destroy any arm you currently have equipped, as will taking any damage while holding down the Breaker Action button.

Command: With a Devil Breaker equipped: Press and hold the Breaker Action button, then release when your right arm sparks with more power.

Break Away

Rip off your current Devil Breaker to create an explosion from the energy held within. Use this to quickly escape from damage.

Command: With a Devil Breaker equipped: Press the assigned Break Away button.

Scapegoat

Attacks won’t stagger you while using a Devil Breaker, or while preparing to use a Break Age. You still take some damage, and your current arm will be destroyed.

Command: Press Breaker Action button while getting attacked, or get attacked while holding the button (with a Devil Breaker equipped)

Overture

A general-purpose Devil Breaker. Blow enemies away with an electric blast or thrust it into foes and detonate from a distance.

Skills

Battery

Fire an electric burst from the palm of the Overture. The direction changes based on the state of the enemy.

Command: With Overture equipped: Press Breaker Action button

Exploder:

Overload the energy inside Overture to turn it into a bomb. Shove it into an enemy and it explodes after a set amount of time. Multiple units can be placed at once. Follow up with a bullet or other attacks to detonate it manually.

Command: Use Overture’s Break Age.

Gerbera

A mobile Devil Breaker letting you cancel attacks with shockwaves or file a converged beam of powerful energy.

Skills

Jocky

Release a shockwave from the palm of the Gerbera. Press the button to attack in front of you. This attack can negate/reflect enemy projectiles. Move in a direction while using this attack to change the shockwave into a thrust to move around.

Command: With Gerbera equipped: Press Breaker Action button (or hold a direction and use Breaker Action button)

Stamen Ray / Petal Ray

While on the ground, Stamen Ray unleashes a powerful blast. In mid-air, Petal Ray will gather shockwaves in your fingertips for a more penetrating laser. Ricochets off of walls and floors around you.

Command: Use Gerbera’s Break Age.

Gerbera GP01

A Devil Breaker coloured by Shoji Kawamori. Excels in mobility, using shockwaves to allow for freer movement.

Skills

Jocky

Release a shockwave form the palm of the Gerbera GP01. While on the ground, press the button to take to the sky, or in mid-air press it to return ot the ground. In mid-air you can move around freely by holding a direction.

Command: With Gerbera GP01 equipped: Press Breaker Action button (or hold a direction and use Breaker Action button)

Punch Line

This Devil Breaker runs rampant, offering powerful strikes and flying rocket punches. You can even mount it to move around.

Skills

Jet Gadget

Launches the Punch Line like a rocket. Press the button after launch to call it back, and hold it when it returns to ride it. While riding, press the gun, melee or breaker button to do tricks, then drop off with the jump button, and blow it up with the Break Away button. You can ride it longer with a higher style rank.

Command: With Punch Line equipped: Hold Breaker Action button during aerial recovery to ride it. Perform manoeuvres with Gun, Melee, Breaker or Jump.

Boost Knuckle

Unleash the Punch Line’s propulsion potential in a haymaker punch. Even large enemies will be knocked back with a clean hit.

Command: Use Punch Line’s Break Age move.

Helter Skelter

This Devil Breaker makes up for a lack of range with superior piercing power. Use it to break through enemy guards.

Skills

Drill RIg

Spin your arm to unleash a penetrating attack that punches through guards and barriers alike.

Command: With Helter Skelter equipped: Tap Breaker repeatedly.

Drill Master

Spin your arm to unleash a penetrating attack. Break through all barriers and guards with a single blow, but the arm will break after this one use.

Command: Use Helter Skelter’s Break Age move.

Tomboy

A Devil Breaker with hidden power that lets you power up Blue Rose and Red Queen. You can adjust the power up duration.

Skills

One Night Stand

Push your weapons past their limits! (IT’S TIME TO GO SUPER SAIYAN!) The Red Queen and Blue Rose are powered up while this ability is activated. Press the Breaker button while activated to turn it off.

Command: With Tomboy equipped: Press Breaker button to activate, press it again to turn it off.

Rendezvous

Power-ups for the Red Queen and Blue Rose last longer the more you charge up. Tomboy won’t be destroyed from enemy attacks while this is activated, but the arm is still lost after a set time.

Command: Use Tomboy’s Break Age.

EX Shot / EX Shot Air / EX Charge Shot

Attach Tomboy to the Blue Rose for a performance boost. Lets you fire powerful penetrating shots. Can be used mid-air and charged with upgrades.

Command: When Tomboy is active: Press the gun button to prep, press it again to fire. If upgraded, you can hold the button to charge Blue Rose even further (hold till arm crackles with max power).

Red Queen Combo EX

Attach Tomboy to the Red Queen for a performance boost. Beast out a ferocious 7-hit sword combo. Each blow changes up your combo, culminating in a power coup de grace if you land each one.

Command: When Tomboy is active: While on the ground, press melee button 7 times.

Red Queen Air EX

Attach Tomboy to the Red Queen for a performance boost. Attack again after the spinning slash to unleash a single blow like a wild animal pouncing on prey.

Command: When Tomboy is active: In mid-air, press melee button twice.

Bloody Queen

Become a whirling demon as the souped-up Red Queen goes on a rampage, taking you along form the ride. Shred any foe in your path, with successful blows opening foes to follow-up attacks. Can be used mid-air with upgrades.

Command: When Tomboy is active: While on ground, press Lock-On + Melee. If used in mid-air, press Melee multiple times to add extra blows.

Buster Arm

A Devil Breaker that can execute a one-hit kill using the Devil Bringer “Buster” technique. Its incredible power makes it fragile and prone to breakage.

Skills

Neo Buster

Reminiscent of the Devil Bringer’s Buster attack. Packed with power, but being attack while executing the move will cause the Buster Arm to shatter.

Command: With Buster Arm equipped: Press Breaker action button.

Super Buster

Attacks won’t cause you to flinch while this is activated, making this easier to execute. However, breaks after one use.

Command: Use Buster Arm’s Break Age move.

Rawhide

Boasts the widest attack range. Use the metal whip for a long-distance strike, or to reel in certain heavy enemies.

Skills

Side Winder

Rawhide changes into countless blades that you can whip around you with the Breaker action button. Press it with the right timing to increase attacks and to strike more enemies.

Command: With Rawhide equipped: While on ground, press Breaker action move 3 times.

Loop The Loop

Stretches Rawhide to its limits, pulling in enemies it connects with and slamming them into the ground. Larger enemies experience a multi-step stab attack instead.

Command: Use Rawhide’s Break Age move.

Ragtime

A Devil Breaker that controls time. Projects a special field that slows all objects within its range.

Skills

Slow Sphere

Fire a sphere that slows anything within its area of influence, including enemies, projectiles and you. Larger foes will break the sphere.

Command: With Ragtime equipped: Press Breaker Action button.

Slow World

Overcomes Ragtime’s limitations (BREAK THROUGH, THE LIMIT!) to slow down everything around you. Enemies, projectiles, fast-moving thorns, nothing escapes its influence.

Command: Use Ragtime’s Break Age move.

Pasta Breaker

An odd Devil Breaker made to help Nero eat pasta. The end spins, but it is not very practical.

Skills

Pasta Jet

Launch the Pasta Breaker as a rocket-propelled projectile to attack enemies. While in flight, the remaining Devil Breakers change order, moving next in line to the end of the magazine.

Command: With Pasta Breaker equipped: Press Breaker action button.

Pasta Mix

Shove the Pasta Breaker into an enemy’s body, where it will explode after a set amount of time. The explosion itself is quite weak, however, you can use your next Devil Breaker and move around until it detonates.

Command: Use Pasta Breaker’s Break Age.

Sweet Surrender

A Devil Breaker made with Kyrie in mind. The pliable materials are gentle on the skin, and it vibrates to sooth away pain.

Skills

Brief Relief

Flip the switch on the Sweet Surrender to make it vibrate. The massage function gradually refills your vitality. You can move around while it’s on, but taking a hit will break it. Press the button again to turn it off.

Command: With Sweet Surrender equipped: Use the Breaker action button

Sweet Release

Jerk Sweet Surrender off your arm for an explosion of healing energy. Restores vitality in an instant, but blowing the entire load at once destroys the arm.

Command: Use Sweet Surrenders Break Age.

Mega Buster

Mega Man’s buster in Devil Breaker form. Specialises in long-range combat, and alters some actions when equipped.

Skills

Mega Shot

Rapid-fire energy bullets form the Mega Buster. You can even run and jump while shooting. A charge builds up over time, and using this skill when it glows will fire a more powerful Mega Shot.

Command: With Mega Buster equipped: Press the Breaker action button.

Mega Charge Shot

Charge the Mega Buster to release a massive ball of destructive energy. This power, penetrating blast can engulf multiple foes, but the strength breaks the arm.

Command: Use Mega Buster’s Break Age.
